Stock Market Anomaly Detection

Financial Markets ML Risk
Problem

Market volatility creates noise that masks genuine signals. Traditional rule-based alerts flag too much or too little, leaving analysts chasing ghosts.

Insight

Anomalies aren't just statistical outliers — they're narrative events. The question isn't what deviated, but why it matters in context.

System

Built a multi-method detection pipeline comparing Z-Score, Isolation Forest, DBSCAN, LSTM, and Autoencoder models. Ensemble approach reduces false positives and surfaces converging signals.

Impact

Produced a comparative framework that lets analysts choose detection sensitivity by use case — regulatory flagging vs. trading signal vs. risk audit.

Generative AI RAG NLP

Residential Complex Knowledge Graph

Knowledge Engineering Graph DB AI
Problem

Structured data about properties, residents, and relationships lives in silos. Queries that cross those silos require custom joins every time.

Insight

Relationships are first-class data. A knowledge graph doesn't just store entities — it makes the connections queryable.

System

Modeled a residential complex domain as a knowledge graph using RDF/graph DB principles. Entities: units, residents, amenities, transactions. Edges encode relationships and constraints.

Impact

Demonstrated how graph-native thinking unlocks query patterns impossible in relational schemas — applicable to any domain with complex relational structures.
